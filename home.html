   January 14th 2013

Waiting for the Spring So We Can Do Our Thing.

So Joelle and I get into the discussion about what we're looking forward to for this year and we have to decide where we want to go travel-wise. I think we have maybe or four viable options given our limited budget thus far. I would nominate Cleveland, Canada, Upstate New York, and Los Angeles. Cleveland is in the race because of Niesha being located there and it's probably less expensive than probably anywhere else. Canada would be fun because we could make it to a French speaking area to practice our burgeoning second language. Upstate New York might be fun because I have family there and I rarely get to see what the rest of New York looks like. It can get quite suburban/ rural in some places. Lastly, Los Angeles would be fun because it's probably a little warmer and we have family and friends in that section of the country that it would be great to see in their own backyards. There are some other options but those are tops in my book. We'll keep you all up to date on our most latest travels.

   January 13th 2013

Watching the Golden Globes Was Great and Brought Back Mel Gibson into My Personal Lexicon!

So Joelle and I were watchingg the Golden Globes and just cracking up at all the hollywood ballyhoo. Tina Fey and Amy Poehler killed it which I was banking on. I saw Argo and agree that it was a solid film. I just think that it came out too early to get that strong push for an uncontested Oscar win. We'll see. I gotta say that I enjoyed Jodie Foster's speech but was heavily distracted by the Mel Gibson sighting and instantly went to youtube to relive his whole recorded phone call debacle that had knocked him down a peg in the public eye. It's hilarious listening to it again! If you don't have issue with coarse obscenity filled rage then have a listen. Also, if you haven't seen it, go watch "The Beaver".
